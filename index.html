About Me

I am a Bachelor of Engineering student at McGill University (Class of 2028), majoring in Computer Engineering with a Minor in Artificial Intelligence. I currently hold a 3.78 GPA.

My work focuses on the intersection of hardware and software. From designing custom PCBs for Mars rovers to writing firmware for aerial telemetry, I love building systems where code interacts with the physical world.

Beyond engineering, my time volunteering with Youths For Hospice has strengthened my commitment to designing technology that prioritizes accessibility and human needs.
